Our story

Eighteen years of moving freight the right way.

Walkadon Logistics LLC was founded on a simple idea: shippers deserve a carrier that treats every load like it's their own. Today we operate 500+ trucks and a nationwide broker network — but that founding principle still runs the company.

Mission

Reliable freight, radically transparent.

We exist to remove friction from the supply chain. That means transparent pricing, real-time visibility, and drivers who show up on time — every time. Our clients don't manage us; they partner with us.

From a single-truck LTL run to a national dedicated program, we bring the same operational discipline, backed by modern technology and a team that answers the phone at 3 AM.
