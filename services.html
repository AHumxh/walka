Capabilities

Every mode. One partner.

Whether you need a dedicated fleet, an LTL solution or a full 3PL program, Walkadon has the trucks, the network and the technology to get it done.

Full Truckload (FTL)

Dedicated 53' dry vans, reefers and flatbeds. Direct point-to-point service with team-driver options for expedited coast-to-coast lanes.

Less-Than-Truckload

Consolidated LTL service with rate-shopped carriers, standardized transit times, and simplified BOL processing.

Warehousing & 3PL

Bonded, climate-controlled warehousing with pick-and-pack, cross-docking, kitting and inventory dashboards.

Expedited & Hot Shot

Priority dispatch for time-critical freight with sprinter vans, straight trucks and team drivers on standby.

Cross-Border Freight

US ↔ Canada and US ↔ Mexico movement with in-house customs brokerage coordination and C-TPAT compliant carriers.

Intermodal

Rail-truck combinations for long-haul lanes — reduced cost and carbon footprint for non-time-sensitive freight.

Dedicated Fleet

Custom fleet solutions with your branding, KPIs and lanes — the reliability of a private fleet without the capex.

Supply Chain Consulting

Lane analysis, carrier vetting, and network design led by senior logistics veterans.

Freight Brokerage

Access to 4,200+ vetted carriers with rate transparency and single-invoice consolidation.
